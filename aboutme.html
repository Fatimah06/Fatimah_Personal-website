Get to Know Me

Throughout my university studies, I had the opportunity to dedicate three months of my time volunteering with the Management Club within the Department of MIS. This experience enabled me to actively contribute to my academic community and broaden my knowledge. This role significantly enriched my knowledge and aided in the development of valuable interpersonal skills. My enthusiasm and professional interests encompass a diverse range of areas, such as system analysis, business analysis, user interface design, and data representation. Currently, I am diligently working on enhancing my programming skills and 3D design abilities.
