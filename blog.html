My Journey in the World of 3D Design

The world of 3D design is an exciting and fascinating realm that opens the doors to creativity and innovative thinking. I can't help but feel passionate about this field and how it contributes to changing the face of art and design. In this blog, I'll share my personal journey in the world of 3D design and how I find inspiration in turning ideas into tangible realities in three dimensions.


Getting Started: It all began with my curiosity about how to create things that look as real as possible in the virtual world. I was inspired by science fiction movies, video games, and 3D applications that captured my imagination. Thus, my journey into learning 3D design commenced.

Exploring Tools: I acquainted myself with a variety of tools and software used in 3D design, such as Blender, Cinema 4D, and ZBrush. I gradually honed my skills through reading, watching online tutorials, and personal experimentation.
